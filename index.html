Buns & Morning Bites

Confucius Steamed Bun / Kongzi Mantou · Dazhi

No. 2, Lane 621, Beian Rd, Zhongshan District — MRT Dazhi

Try the pumpkin-cheese or longan mantou (南瓜起司 / 桂圓). (The longan is smoke-dried — pleasantly smoky, but a bit of an acquired taste.)

Doupuzi · Neihu (Kangning branch)

No. 20-1, Sec. 3, Kangning Rd, Neihu District — MRT Donghu

Try the soybean pudding (豆花) with taro balls, brown-sugar pearls & handmade brown-sugar gel (黑糖粿), plus the steamed buns.

Seafood

Congee Kingdom · Dazhi

No. 567, Beian Rd, Zhongshan District — across from MRT Dazhi

Try the milkfish congee (虱目魚粥).

The congee comes out absurdly hot, so be careful. Near Kongzi Mantou if you want a bun for dessert.

Map ↗

Addiction Aquatic Development · Zhongshan

No. 18, Alley 2, Lane 410, Minzu E Rd, Zhongshan District — taxi is easiest

A seafood market with a standing sushi bar.

Not sure how to order? This walkthrough of Addiction Aquatic sorts out the stations and the standing bar.

Cash 7am–10:30pm Map ↗

Sushi, generally: just walk into any place that looks good — they're all surprisingly good.

A Walk

Zhishan Yan Huiji Temple · Shilin

No. 26, Lane 326, Sec. 1, Zhicheng Rd, Shilin District — MRT Zhishan

Walk ~200 stone steps up a little jungly knoll, past the Eighteen Arhats (十八羅漢) on their animal mounts and the old Qing-era fort gates, to a hilltop temple.

Open 6am–7pm ~30–45 min round trip Map ↗

Eat slowly, and take the long way home.
